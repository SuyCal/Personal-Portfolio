Project Reflection

Throughout the process of creating this personal portfolio, I have gained valuable insights and experiences in the realms of web design and development. From conceptualizing the layout to implementing various HTML and CSS elements, this project has been an incredible learning opportunity.


As I embarked on this journey, I realized the importance of meticulous planning and organization. By sketching wireframes and defining clear objectives, I was able to create a website that is both visually appealing and user-friendly.


In conclusion, this project has not only honed my skills in web design and development but has also instilled a sense of accomplishment and pride in my work. I am eager to continue refining my abilities and exploring new technologies in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

Challenges

The main challenge I faced throughout this project was effectively adapting my website to different screen sizes and refresh rates. And accounting for mobile use, it was more difficult to adapt the sizes, but I tried my best to circumvent but still i had some bugs.

Along the way, I encountered challenges that pushed me to think critically and develop innovative solutions. For example, incorporating interactive features and optimizing page load times were essential aspects that required careful consideration and testing. Moreover, I took the initiative to learn about search engine optimization (SEO) and implemented best practices to improve my website'svisibility and ranking.


Successes

The successes I got were mainly in formatting the web pages and the UI/UX design in general. I tried to simplify my website a lot, while retaining the functionalities of such. The simplistic design really beautifies the website to another extent and gives it a more sophisticated look.

The use of AI, such as ChatGPT, also helped me a lot in shaping my webpages and giving them a more professional tone in general. It helped me a lot in terms of compiling and error-seeking in my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also used it in terms of idea-seeking for my webpages and for answering trivial questions that needed specific answers.

Also, with a simple line of code, I was able to make the contact froms on the Contact page work, and now everytime someone fills in the form and submits it, I recieve the inputs.
